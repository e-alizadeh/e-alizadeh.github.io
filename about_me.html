About Me

I'm a data scientist at VIA. Before joining VIA in 2018, I was a Data Analysis Engineer at CanmetENERGY research center, Natural Resources Canada (NRCan), where he developed a graphical user interface toolbox for conducting data analysis and causality analysis for complex industrial processes. Prior to that, Esmaeil was a graduate research assistant at Concordia University. His research focused on developing control strategies and fault diagnosis of wind turbines using machine learning techniques.
I got my bachelor of science degree in Electrical Engineering with the distinction of Highest Honors from Honors Program at Qatar University. Then, I moved to Montreal, Canada and received my master of applied science degree in Electrical and Computer Engineering from Concordia University.
